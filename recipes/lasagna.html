Easy, home-made lasagna

    General info:
  • Total time: 2h50m
    • Preparation time: 1h25m
    • Cooking time: 1h15m
    • Additional: 10m
  • Serving: 8 people
  • Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Ground meat: 0.5 pound of ground pork, 0.5 pound of ground beef.
  • Onion: A diced onion.
  • Canned tomatoes: A can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Parsley: 2 tablespoons.
  • Garlic: One clove.
  • Sugar: Just a dash of sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings: Dried basil, oregano, salt, pepper. To your likings.
  • Lasagna noodle: Oven-ready noodle works fine.
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese, parmesean, shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
    • Cook the meat until brown. Then add onions and continue cooking until onion is translucent.
    • Add tomatoes, sugar and all spices and seasonings.
  2. Cook the noodles: cook the noodles in boiling, lightly salted water until al dente.
